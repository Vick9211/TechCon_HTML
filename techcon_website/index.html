TechCon 2024 Conference is an annual event that takes place yearly. This year's event will take place at Sarit Centre, which is located at Westlands, Kenya. The event will see stakeholders from various Tech giants across the world come together. The event will provide a great opportunity for stakeholders and attendees to network and engage with like-minded individuals. Besides that, there willbe fireside chats with some of the best reknown Tech leaders who have been making a great impact to the world. You don't want to miss this one!
